# Phase 6 Documentation: AI Investment Intelligence Engine

## Objective
The AI Investment Intelligence Engine takes a structured `ResearchBundle` and runs Gemini via LangChain.js to produce a comprehensive, structured `InvestmentAnalysis` report. It validates scores, checks SWOT counts, checks risk matrices, and performs auto-retries on schema or constraint violations without emitting markdown text.

## Architecture
The engine is structured into separate functional files to maintain isolation:
- **Zod Schema**: `schemas/investment.schema.ts` implements the validation contract.
- **Parser**: `analysis/output-parser.ts` handles markdown stripping and Zod validation.
- **Engine**: `analysis/investment-engine.ts` compiles prompt messages, calls Gemini, and runs verification loops.
- **Guardrails**:
  - `scoring.ts`: Verifies score boundaries and overall consistency.
  - `risk.ts`: Checks the completeness of risk rating dimensions.
  - `swot.ts`: Verifies SWOT arrays contain at least 3 points.
  - `recommendation.ts`: Ensures consistency between recommendation enums and scores.
  - `summary.ts`: Enforces the 250-word length constraint.
  - `confidence.ts`: Penalizes confidence scores dynamically based on missing data.

## Scoring Methodology
Seven core scores are generated by the engine (from 0 to 100):
1. **Business Quality**: Market position, moat strength, operating efficiency.
2. **Financial Health**: Margins, debt-to-equity, liquidity, ROE.
3. **Growth**: Revenue trends, sector expansion, product launches.
4. **Risk**: Inverse safety indicator (High risk = high value).
5. **Competitive Advantage**: Switching costs, network effects, cost advantages.
6. **Valuation**: Pricing models (P/E, PEG ratios).
7. **Overall Investment Score**: Weighted score indicating attractiveness. Must match recommendations.

## Recommendation Thresholds
- **Strong Buy**: Overall Score >= 85, high confidence.
- **Buy**: Overall Score 70-84.
- **Hold**: Overall Score 45-69.
- **Avoid**: Overall Score 30-44.
- **Strong Avoid**: Overall Score < 30.

## Error Recovery Retry Loop
If the generative model returns invalid JSON, fails Zod schema validation, or violates any of our analytical guardrails (e.g. word count exceeds 250 words, or scores are inconsistent), the engine intercepts the error, appends a correction message detailing the specific validation failure, and retries the call. The engine allows a maximum of 2 retries (3 total attempts) before raising a `ValidationError`.
